Past Clients

UNDP’s Business Call to Action Programme
Locations: Indonesia, India, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Cameroon, Benin, Burkina Faso, Mexico

Business Call to Action is a UNDP initiative recognizing and advancing inclusive businesses to help achieve the Sustainable Development Goals.

Arthify worked with 22 inclusive-business members of BCtA – developing Theory of Change (ToCs) models and impact measurement metrics; implementing mobile data-collection tools and analytics; and authoring twelve case-studies: L’Occitane, GE Healthcare, Medtronic, Tolaro Global, East West Seeds Indonesia, Cemex, East Bali Cashews and Mahindra Home Finance, amongst others.

Abdul Latif Jameel Poverty Action Lab
Locations: India

JPAL is founded by Nobel Laureates Esther Duflo and Abhijit Banerjee.  Its mission is to reduce poverty by ensuring that policy is informed by scientific evidence -- through research, policy outreach, and training.

JPAL South Asia’s first foray into using mobile-based survey work and field-staff management was done using Arthify’s Fieldata platform.  Arthify developed mobile-based survey tools for JPAL’s impact study of elections in the urban slums of Delhi, and a maternal literacy project in Rajasthan and Bihar.

Living Goods
Locations: Uganda, Kenya

Living Goods is a social enterprise that aims to ensure that there is a digitally empowered community health worker (CHW) in every community. Local women and men are transformed into frontline health workers who can deliver on-demand, life-saving care to families in need.

Arthify had developed an SMS-based communications management platform with smart-phone based data collection forms for surveys and Point-of-Sale data integration.

Innovations for Poverty Action
Locations: Mexico, Nigeria, Uganda, Ghana

Innovations for Poverty Action (IPA) is a global social research organization. It carries out Randomized Control Trials (RCTs) of policy and program interventions globally.

Arthify’s Fieldata platform was used by IPA for multiple RCT projects. Arthify assisted in survey designing, programming, testing and implementation of the impact assessment studies related to agriculture, education and health projects.

The World Bank
Locations: India

The World Bank Group works in every major area of development. It provides a wide array of financial products and technical assistance to developing countries, sharing and applying innovative knowledge and solutions to the challenges they face.

Arthify provided the technology platform to support a country-wide Social Pension Survey sanctioned by the World Bank on behalf of the Ministry of Rural Development, India.

The Clear Initiative
Locations: Global

The Clear Initiative promotes better development outcomes through increased evaluation capacity, learning, and the use of evidence.

Arthify (Fieldata) was the leading author of the CLEAR Initiative’s guidebook on mobile-based monitoring and evaluation, and it participated in training for CLEAR participants.
